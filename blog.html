JNTUA secures Asia’s rank between 301-350

Written by anusha on 14/20

JNTU-Anantapur has secured rankings between 301 and 350 announced by the Times Higher Education, Asia University’s Best Universities 2018 list, Vice-Chancellor Prof S Srinivas Kumar announced here on Wednesday. He said JNTUA has bagged the third rank among the government varsities in Andhra Pradesh. These ranks have been announced based on the university’s research, faculty and abilities of the students, according to the VC. These ranks would pave the way for the faculty and students to undertake research and higher studies. The VC, Rector Subba Rao, Registrar S Krishnayya, DIRP Prof G Prashanthi thanked everyone for JNTU securing the recognition.
